This projects showcases an online dashboard hosting data from Thames21 Socio-Ecological Data repo. Users are invited to explore a map-based visualisation of social and environmental data linked to local rivers in the Thames river basin district, learn about how water quality affects well-being, and interact with the dashboard by contributing their own sentiment to the map.
