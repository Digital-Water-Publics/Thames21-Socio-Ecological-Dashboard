How do twitter users feel about London rivers?

Welcome!

This map shows social media sentiment about 545 water bodies in the Thames river basin district. Social media sentiment is based on more than 3 million tweets during the last 15 years that mention a water body by name. The map displays sentiment polarity, which means that Twitter users have either expressed negative (green) or positive (blue) views about their local water bodies.

You can zoom in or out to switch between three different scales:
- management catchment
- operational catchment
- water body
